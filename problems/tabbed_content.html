Support Characters

Soraka

Taric

Leona

Soraka is old standby, the heavyweight definition of a support character. She uses her abilities to regenerate allies, and later farm creep waves. She can be very squishy and a tempting target for enemy hard AP nuke champions, so be sure to stock up on some armor, MR, and health, and try to stay in the back!

Taric is a great support character, in that he offers a full complement of supporting bonuses. Heals, stuns, auras: you name it, he has it. All this wrapped up in a tanky little support champion that is truly, truly... Outrageous.

Leona takes tanky support to the next level. Her base armor and health progression plus an enormous amount of interrupt and stun capability makes her a great companion for that ADC who loves to be aggro. Play her with some skill and guts, and you'll be securing bot lane in no time.
